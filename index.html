Weapon Tiers and Backstory

Russia's failed experiments of brining the dead to life have escape infecting the poor locals and some major cities in the north east part of Russia the zombies come in 3 forms crawlers the best to mess with, Allfours crazy fast zombies that run on all fours and last of all the normal zombie slow an stupid. Only a certain amount of survivors live on these parts of Russia so I have created a guide to help those lived to regain Russia again.

Tips on how to survive

1. Dont Trust Anybody
2. Always Carry a Melee Weapon For Zombies
3. Sound Attracts Zombies be Quiet
4. Stay Out of Sight
5. Try to Camoflauge Dont Wear Bright Colours
6. Always Carry Bandages Rags Work Too
7. Dont Wander the Roads
8. Watch Out For Agro Zombies
9. Dont Shoot Unless Nessesary
10. Dont Die
